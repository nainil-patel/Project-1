The next day, curiosity pulled him back. He returned to the counter.

“What movie?” the ticket seller asked.

“Don (1978),” the critic replied.

“That will be 10 credits.”

The seller handed him another special ticket and muttered, “Enjoy the experience.”

The film played again. But was it slower? Was it… different?
